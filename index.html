Company Summary

Divine Innovation Hub (DIH) is a dynamic and inclusive hub created to support the livelihood of young entrepreneurs and women artisans in the informal sector.

Our mission is to identify, develop, and shape the skills of individuals, empowering them to become successful entrepreneurs. Through a range of comprehensive programs and support services, DIH aims to foster entrepreneurship, promote business formalization, and create a supportive ecosystem for sustainable economic growth.

Develop livelihood programs for

  1. Youth
  2. Women
  3. Vulnerable
